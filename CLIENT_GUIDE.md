# Haifa HiveMind — Setup Guide

Welcome! This is your own private research assistant. It runs **only on your
computer** — nothing you upload or ask ever leaves your PC.

You only have to do the setup **once**. It takes about 15 minutes (most of it is
just waiting for downloads).

---

## Step 1 — Install Ollama (the AI engine)

1. Go to **https://ollama.com/download**
2. Download the version for your computer (Windows) and run the installer.
3. Click through — all default options are fine.

That's it. Ollama runs quietly in the background.

## Step 2 — Download the AI models

Find the file **`setup_models`** we gave you and **double-click it**.
A black window opens and downloads the AI brain (about 6 GB). Leave it until it
says **"Models ready"**, then close it.

> Only needed once. This is what makes the AI work offline forever after.

## Step 3 — Install Haifa HiveMind

Double-click **`Haifa HiveMind Setup.exe`** and follow the installer.
When it finishes, a **Haifa HiveMind** icon appears on your desktop.

---

## Using it every day

Open **Haifa HiveMind** from your desktop. You'll see:

### 🔌 The power buttons (top-left)
- **On** — starts the AI. Do this first.
- **Pause** — frees your graphics card when you take a break (resumes in seconds).
- **Off** — shuts the AI down completely so it uses no resources.

> Tip: **Pause** or **Off** when you're done, so the AI isn't using your GPU/RAM.

### 📄 Add your documents (left panel)
Drag research papers, Word/PDF files, or even **screenshots** onto the
**"Drop papers"** box. The AI reads and remembers them automatically. When a file
shows **indexed**, it's ready to be used in answers.

### 💬 Ask questions (main area)
Type your question and press Enter. Answers are based on *your* documents and
show which files they came from. You can also attach a screenshot to a question
using the 📎 button.

### 👍 Teach it (under every answer)
- **Good** — tells the AI that answer was right. It saves these to get smarter.
- **Not what I wanted** — the AI will rethink. You can add a note about what you
  actually meant, and it will try again or ask you a clarifying question.

### 🧠 Make it smarter (Self-Improvement panel)
After you've approved enough answers, the **Fine-tune now** button lights up.
Click it (ideally when you're not using the PC) and the AI trains itself on your
approved answers. This runs in the background.

## Common questions

**Is my data private?**
Yes — 100%. Everything runs on your computer. There is no internet account, no
cloud, no data sharing.

**It says "AI is OFF"?**
Press the **On** button in the top-left and wait a few seconds.

**It's slow the first time I ask?**
The first question after turning On loads the AI into memory. After that it's
fast.

**Do I need internet?**
Only for the one-time setup (Steps 1–2). After that it works fully offline.
